## 46. What did your database design make easy, and what was difficult?

The database design made it easy to connect students, programs, instructors, attendance, and payments using foreign keys. It also made it easy to create reports using JOIN, GROUP BY, HAVING, and aggregate functions.

The most difficult part was designing the relationships correctly and ensuring that foreign keys and constraints maintained data integrity.
